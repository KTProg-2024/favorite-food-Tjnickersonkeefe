My Favorite Foods

Maki

Entree

Maki is a roll of usually rice, fish, and some sort of vegetable, in a seaweed wrap, sized just right to fit in your mouth.

Chocolate

Desert

Chocolate is a mix of a bean powder and milk, vanilla, and sugar. It can be shaped into many forms, and combined with many things to make many different recipes.

Pizza

Entree

Pizza is a great food because it perfectly blends some of the standards of western food. Tomatoes, cheese, bread. Meat, sometimes. And it combines them together perfectly.

Chips

Snack

Chips are a great snack, because they keep the flavor thats mixed in perfectly with the crispness of the chip, adding just the right mix. Can even be used in sandwiches to add crunch to an otherwise soft sandwich.

Steak

Entree

Steak is an amazing entree, the pure meat without any disturbences other than seasoning shows how amazing the pure protein is.
